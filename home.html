Welcome to the University of Chitral

Generate and Disseminate Knowledge

To serve the society by nurturing the students with a delicate intellectual, cultural, ethical, and humane sensitivity through a pleasant blend of ancient and modern wisdom; to foster a scientific temper and to promote professional and technological expertise.

Pivotal to this vision is a commitment to contributing in local, national and international development, concomitantly adhering to the dictates of our social ethics, traditions and environment.

Vice Chancellor’s Message

Welcome to the University of Chitral—a newly established public sector university in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. Our mission goes beyond simply classroom teaching and basic research—we strive to provide an academic environment that encourages dialogue, critical thinking, innovation, and respect for diversity and inclusiveness.

Our faculty is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement—bringing real-world experience into the classroom.
